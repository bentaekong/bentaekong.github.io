My Code of Ethics #1

One thing that I can truly say is that I definitely place a high value on grades. This is probably why I am even writing or in this case, typing this paper. Not only that, I can also say the reason why I place such high importance on grades is that because I believe that it can truly affect the college I can go to. As a result, I also value the place I go to after highschool.

My Code of Ethics #2

One of my main aspirations is to become rich. However, it does not mean that I also want to become famous. The reason why I want to become rich is to use my wealth for improvements in society. I truly believe that equality is key to harmony. Everybody in life, rich or poor, should still get proper items like education. The reason why I have this kind of mentality is because I know how it feels to be in a low-income family. I hope that the generations would below would not have to go through the trouble of not being have equal educational opportunities. This also shows that I would be willing to work in order to achieve my goal. In order to get where I want to be, I have to work for it. Which shows that I can be hard working at times.

My Code of Ethics #3

I think of myself as somebody with potential of success. I always have high personal expectations of myself because I know that I can always do better. Not only that, I was raised upon the idea that there was always room for improvements. Through my experience living under tiger parents, I can also relate to other students (commonly asian) that also lived through the idea that there was never such thing as "good enough." Thus, that idea grew upon me.
